Hugo's collection methods are counterintuitive on a date-descending list: $pages.Next . -> the NEWER post (appears earlier in the collection) $pages.Prev . -> the OLDER post (appears later in the collection) The stock theme wired Next->"« Prev" and Prev->"Next »", which put newer posts behind "Prev" and older posts behind "Next" — backwards for a reader. Here "« Prev" walks back in time (older) and "Next »" forward (newer). */ -}} {{- $pages := where site.RegularPages "Type" "in" site.Params.mainSections }} {{- if and (gt (len $pages) 1) (in $pages . ) }}
